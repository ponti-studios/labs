# Infinite Scroll — Product Brief

## Purpose

Explore how repetition, speed, masking, and image rhythm can turn a collection into an atmosphere.

## Core model

Several continuous image fields move at different or opposing rhythms. The viewer does not need to inspect each image individually; the collection works as a composition over time.

## Core loop

Enter the field, watch the rhythm establish itself, pause or linger, and notice relationships between movement, repetition, color, and image.

## Novelty

The collection is not content inside a scrolling interface. The scrolling itself is the compositional medium.

## Discovery and proof

The visitor should stop thinking “these are images scrolling” and begin experiencing: “the collection has become a moving environment.” The concept works when the piece holds attention through atmosphere before the mechanism is understood.

## Non-goals

This is not an infinite-feed product, content browser, or substitute for a portfolio gallery. If the collection has no curatorial reason to exist, the mechanism is not enough.
