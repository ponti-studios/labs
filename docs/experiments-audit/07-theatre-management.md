# Theatre Management — Rewrite Checklist

**Product brief:** [Theatre Management](07-theatre-management-brief.md)  
**Source:** `apps/labyrinth/app/routes/theatre-management/`

## Preserve

- [ ] Preserve the financial model and pure calculation layer.
- [ ] Preserve lineup allocation, seasonality, pricing, concessions, and health logic.
- [ ] Preserve the immediate feedback loop from decision to outcome.

## Remove

- [ ] Remove generic dashboard treatment that hides the strategic tension.
- [ ] Remove metric presentation that gives every number equal importance.
- [ ] Remove controls that expose values without explaining their consequences.

## Rebuild

- [ ] Make the programming-versus-survival tension the central reading of the page.
- [ ] Make the primary financial consequence unmistakable.
- [ ] Make allocation choices and their downstream effects easy to follow.
- [ ] Present the P&L according to financial reading conventions.
- [ ] Decide which single comparison or trend best proves the model.

## Verify

- [ ] A visitor understands that cultural choices change economic outcomes.
- [ ] Every major control produces a legible consequence.
- [ ] The model remains credible without requiring an explanation of implementation.

## Complete when

- [ ] The tool feels like a decision model, not a form with financial numbers.
