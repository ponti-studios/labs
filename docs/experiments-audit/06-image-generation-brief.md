# Image Generation — Product Brief

## Purpose

Expose the chain between visual intention, structured description, language, and image.

## Core model

The user makes a set of visual choices. Those choices become a structured configuration, then a compiled description, then an image. The chain is the subject; the generated image is its visible consequence.

## Core loop

Describe an image, inspect the compiled language, generate the result, compare intention with output, and refine the description.

## Novelty

It treats prompting as an act of visual direction and translation rather than a mysterious text box.

## Discovery and proof

The visitor should recognize: “This image is the result of a chain of explicit creative decisions.” The concept works when they can trace at least one visible choice from the form into the prompt and from the prompt into the image.

## Non-goals

This is not a general image-generation platform, batch production tool, or form-builder showcase. Additional features are out of scope unless they clarify the translation from thought to image.
