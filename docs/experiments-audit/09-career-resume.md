# Career Resume — Rewrite Checklist

**Product brief:** [Career Resume](09-career-resume-brief.md)  
**Source:** `apps/labyrinth/app/routes/experiments.career-resume-animated.tsx`

## Preserve

- [ ] Preserve the short narrative sequence and confident voice.
- [ ] Preserve timed progression, manual navigation, spring entrance, and progress context.
- [ ] Preserve the anti-resume structure.

## Remove

- [ ] Remove dead CTAs and dead links.
- [ ] Remove content or timing that exists only because it is hardcoded to one presentation.
- [ ] Remove navigation behavior that obscures the narrative on smaller screens.

## Rebuild

- [ ] Make the narrative argument legible from first frame to final frame.
- [ ] Decide whether this is a personal artifact or a reusable resume format.
- [ ] Make timing serve content rather than impose uniform pacing.
- [ ] Make every action perform a real, understandable function.
- [ ] Define pause, replay, keyboard, touch, and reduced-motion behavior.

## Verify

- [ ] The visitor remembers a point of view, not just a list of credentials.
- [ ] The sequence works when manually navigated and when allowed to play.
- [ ] The final action and contact paths are real.

## Complete when

- [ ] The resume feels experienced as a story rather than read as a document.
