# Career Resume — Product Brief

## Purpose

Turn a resume into a short interactive argument that earns attention through pacing, voice, and point of view.

## Core model

The visitor moves through a sequence of interruption, identity, value, evidence, and invitation. Order and timing create meaning that a static document cannot.

## Core loop

Enter the narrative, follow the progression, choose whether to advance or let it play, and arrive at a clear invitation to continue the relationship.

## Novelty

It treats a resume as a performed encounter rather than a document to be searched for completeness.

## Discovery and proof

The visitor should feel that they have met a person and understood a point of view—not merely scanned credentials. The concept works when the visitor remembers the argument and personality before the individual facts.

## Non-goals

This is not a complete employment record or a reusable resume platform by default. It must first succeed as a narrative artifact.
