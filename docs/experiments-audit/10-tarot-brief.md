# Tarot — Product Brief

## Purpose

Create a daily pause through scarcity, anticipation, interpretation, and return.

## Core model

One card is available for the user's local day. The card provides a prompt, reading, and reflection material; persistence gives the encounter continuity and prevents endless consumption.

## Core loop

Return, anticipate, draw once, read, reflect, and carry one idea into the day.

## Novelty

It uses digital scarcity and curated meaning to create a modest ritual rather than an endless content feed or an authoritative oracle.

## Discovery and proof

The visitor should understand: “The value is not certainty. The value is having a shape for attention today.” The concept works when the draw creates anticipation and leaves something to reflect on after the page is closed.

## Non-goals

This is not an objective divination engine, content archive, or generic AI result page. AI and curated material support the ritual but do not replace it.
